## Unitary Matrix
> [!definition|] Unitary Matrix
> Let $Q\in \mathbb{C}^{n}$. Q is an unitary matrix if columns of $Q$ are orthonormal.

> [!remark|]
> The eigenvectors of an orthogonal matrix form an unitary matrix.
