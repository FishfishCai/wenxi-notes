## Unitary Matrix
> [!definition|] Unitary Matrix
> Let $Q\in \mathbb{C}^{n,n}$. Q is an unitary matrix if columns of $Q$ are orthonormal.

> [!remark|]
> The eigenvectors of an orthogonal matrix form an unitary matrix.

> [!remark|]
> [[Real Matrix#^03854c]], [[Real Matrix#^727a78]], [[Real Matrix#^4c69c4]], [[Real Matrix#^a712cb]], [[Real Matrix#^f47e7a]] and [[Real Matrix#^b5daf5]] follow if we change $^{\top}$ to $^{*}$.
